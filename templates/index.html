Story behind Ruff

Hey, I’m Irfan.

This is a web companion to the original Ruff desktop app — built as a lighter, browser‑friendly way to stash ideas while giving credit to the app that inspired it.

The look is a love letter to the ’90s Recess cartoon (my childhood favorite), so the doodle‑notebook vibe is intentional. Short story: I wanted something fast, friendly, and fun — and that’s what Ruff is.
